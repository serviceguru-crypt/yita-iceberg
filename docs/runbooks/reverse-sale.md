# Reverse Sale

1. Open the completed order.
2. Select `Reverse`.
3. Choose the reversal type.
4. Enter item quantities, refund amount, credit correction, and reason.
5. Submit request.
6. Admin/super-admin reviews and approves.
7. Admin/super-admin completes the approved reversal.
8. Confirm stock movement and financial correction records.

Refunds are internal records only unless an external payment process is completed separately.
