# Stock Receipt

1. Confirm supplier document/reference outside the app.
2. Sign in as branch manager/admin.
3. Select the correct branch.
4. Open `/inventory/receipts/new`.
5. Enter products, quantities, and unit costs.
6. Review totals before posting.
7. Confirm inventory and `inventoryFinancials` updated.
8. Confirm a `stock_received` movement was created.

Do not post a receipt to correct a sale reversal; use the reversal workflow.
